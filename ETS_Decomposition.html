We can clearly see the higher than linear growing trend and seasonality waves towards the end. Let's now use the seasonal decompose function that we earlier imported from statsmodels to separate its components

In [22]:
decomp = seasonal_decompose(airline['Passengers(in thousands)'], model='mul') # mul is for multiplicative model
decomp.plot();

Here the first graph shows the stretched version of our regular data, second is the trend component, third is the seasonality component and fourth is the error/noise/residual.
